About Our Mission

1Safe Click was founded by high school students with a passion for technology and a commitment to making the digital world safer for everyone. We recognized that cybersecurity education is often targeted at professionals or tech-savvy individuals, leaving many vulnerable to online threats.

Our mission is simple: to make cybersecurity knowledge accessible to people of all ages and backgrounds. Whether you're a senior citizen learning to use email, a parent trying to keep your children safe online, or a small business owner protecting customer data, we provide resources tailored to your needs.

Through workshops, educational materials, and community outreach, we're building a network of cyber-aware citizens who can protect themselves and help others stay safe in our increasingly connected world.

Cybersecurity Resources

Fun, interactive games and activities that teach children about online safety in an engaging way. Perfect for ages 7-12.

Tips for protecting privacy, managing digital reputation, and avoiding common pitfalls on social platforms.

Step-by-step instructions for securing your home Wi-Fi network and connected devices.
